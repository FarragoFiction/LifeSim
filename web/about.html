• What is it???

    LifeSim is an homage to Problem Sleuth's Game of Life. I am confident that this is the single best fan Problem Sleuth anything in existance. ... And if I'm wrong I'm hella interested to hear about my rivals.

    That said, the narrative mechanic here is dead fucking simple. You can see it right on the scene cards. Scenes have trigger conditions (like a stat being greater than, less than or equal to some value) and have results when they finish.


  • What do I do with it???

    LifeSim has two core gameplay loops:
    1. Turn scenes on and off and see how that changes the Protagonist of LifeSim. Scenes chosen effect what is possible, but are not guaranteed to happen. Will they go to college, find love, die in a fire, raise alien babies? Who knows!!!
    2. Collecting more scenes to use to torment help the Protagonist. Scenes can be automatically unlocked in many places on Farrago Fiction, or you may find other ways to access them...
